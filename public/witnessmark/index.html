What this does and does not claim

This page can help show that specific input produced a specific SHA-256 fingerprint. In LOOPtLOOP internal mode, it can also record that the mark was created while this browser and a simulated local agent adapter mutually verified a time-bound co-presence loop.

It does not prove ownership, authorship, truth, legality, patent priority, external identity, absolute time, or legally valid consent. It may assist future identification, comparison, interpretation, timestamping, or witness.
