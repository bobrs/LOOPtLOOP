Non-claims

This prototype does not prove more than visible participation in a live loop.

  • It does not prove the truth of what the video shows.
  • It does not prove identity, authorship, or who controlled the camera.
  • It does not prove the final file was never edited after capture.
  • It proves only that the recording visibly contained a live LOOPtLOOP provenance signal during capture.
